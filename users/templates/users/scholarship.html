Scholarship and Privileges


Entrance Examination

Valedictorian and Salutatorian – free tuition fee for one year with cash incentives Honorable Mention – discount on tuition fee for one semester following this schedule:

1st = 35%
2nd = 30%
3rd = 25%
4th = 20%
5th = 15%

Academic Scholar

General Ave. 1.0 – 1.20 – Full Free Scholar
General Ave. 1.21 – 1.45 – 50% on tuition fee

With brothers and sisters in CM (with lowest tuition fee)
2 = each will get 10% discount
3 = one will get 50% discount
4 = one will get free tuition fee
5 = one will get free tuition fee and one will get 50% discount
6 = two will get free tuition fee
7 = two will get free tuition fee and one will get 50% discount

Children /Husband /Wife of UV full time faculty – 50% on tuition.

Government Employees /Teachers – 30% discount on tuition fee

CM Faculty – 6 units free for undergraduate, 9 units for graduate school

Early Enrolment Discount – 10% for old student who will enroll three weeks before the regular enrolment for the next semester / term.

NOTE: If more than one discount is applicable to a student applicant, the maximum accumulated discount should not be over 40%.

Athletic and Cultural Grants

Free tuition fee for varsity players in basketball, volleyball, soccer, tennis, chess, scrabble, boxing and other sports. Dance troupe members, singers, theatre, and drama guild members, and those who are gifted in painting also enjoy this privileged.
