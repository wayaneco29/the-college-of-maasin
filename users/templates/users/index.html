Mission

The College of Maasin, with the integration of instruction, research and extension, commits itself in seeking a life of faith, learning and action to develop people into becoming God-loving citizens with integrity in character, intellectually performance, creatively aware and responsive to the needs and aspirations of people for the realization of a just, free and responsible Christian social order.

Vision

The College of Maasin as a dynamic learning institution that commits its life resources and ministry toward the development of persons. Nurtured by faith and by the liberating process of excellent learning toward wholistic and creative action for social renewal.

Motto

“Nisi Dominus Frustra” (Without God in Vain)

This motto is a solid affirmation of our dependence on God’s grace and mercy. So we respond in gratitude and trust in our abilities to become God’s stewards of creation.
